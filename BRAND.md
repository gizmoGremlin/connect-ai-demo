# Connect AI Brand Reference

## Logo

Minimal abstract 3-square logo: three equal small squares touching at their vertices/corners. Top and bottom squares are vertically aligned, middle square is offset to the left. The squares are NOT spaced apart — they lightly touch at the corners, forming a tight connected geometric shape. Subtle inset engraving, monochrome silver-on-silver, premium industrial design.

### Prompt-friendly description

> Three equal small squares touching at their corners — top and bottom squares vertically aligned, middle square offset to the left. The squares touch at vertices with zero gap between them, forming one tight connected geometric mark. Engraved/inset into the metal surface.

## Product

A small thin rectangular plate attached to the back of an iPhone. The plate is smaller than the phone, covering only the lower two-thirds of the phone's back, leaving the iPhone's camera lenses exposed at the top. It connects through the iPhone's USB-C port at the bottom. The plate is very thin and flush against the phone.
